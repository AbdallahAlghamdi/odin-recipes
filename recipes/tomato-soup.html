Tomato soup

Description

This tomato soup recipe is simple, quick, and perfect to make when tomatoes are ripe in gardens and farmers' markets for a delicious summertime treat

Ingredients

  • Chopped fresh tomatoes
  • Chicken broth
  • Cloves garlic
  • Slice of onion
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • white sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onions, clove and broth
  2. Run the mixture through a food mill into large bowl
  3. Make a roux
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season
